🎨 Thumbs Up for Art & Music

📖 Text Map — Read this first!

📝
TEXT TYPE
"Thumbs Up for Art and Music" is a persuasive text — the author is trying to convince you that art and music are important subjects at school.
🎨
REASON 1 — ART
Art is important because it makes you think creatively. You learn to use your imagination to make new things.
🎵
REASON 2 — MUSIC
Music is important because you can play with others and learn about teamwork. It also helps you learn about other cultures.
💡
AUTHOR'S OPINION
The author believes both art AND music should stay in schools. These subjects help students grow as thinkers and team players.
