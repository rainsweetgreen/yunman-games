🐮 The Cow and the Tiger

📖 Story Map — Read this first!

🏠
SETTING
Bala the cow lives in a shadowy forest — that's her home! Every day she walks to a nearby grassy field to graze (eat grass).
🐯
PROBLEM
One day, a tiger blocks Bala's path and says: "I will eat your calf!"
Bala says: "Please wait — let me go feed my calf first. I promise I will come back to the forest."
🌿
WHAT BALA DOES
Bala goes to the grassy field, feeds her calf, then returns to the shadowy forest — even though she could have run away! She kept her promise.
😲
TURNING POINT
The tiger is amazed. He never expected Bala to actually come back. Her honesty and bravery move his heart.
💛
MORAL
Always keep your promises — even when it's hard or scary. The tiger sets Bala and her calf free because of her honesty.
